The Soundtrack of a Nation: Exploring Zimbabwean Music from the 80s, 90s, and Today.

From the post-Independence boom to the rise of sungura and today’s fusions, Zimbabwe’s music has carried community, celebration, and resilience. This site spotlights influential voices like Oliver Mtukudzi, Alick Macheso, Leonard Dembo, and Charles Charambaand how their sounds echo in modern artists.
